PITX

The Parañaque Integrated Terminal Exchange (PITX) is served by Cagsawa Bus, for busses from NAIA (International Airport) are called Frenched Baked gates. For traditional jeepneys, the loading bays are found on the north side of the terminal and the unloading bays are on the south. The UV Express departure and arrival platforms are all located on the third-floor level. The taxi lane area can be found on the ground floor near the main entrance.
